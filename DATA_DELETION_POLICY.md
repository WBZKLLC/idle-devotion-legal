# Data Deletion Request Policy for Idle Devotion

**Last Updated:** January 2026

This Data Deletion Request Policy explains how users of Idle Devotion can request deletion of personal data associated with their account.

---

## 1. Data Subject to Deletion

Depending on how you use the App, data associated with your account may include:
- Account identifiers
- Gameplay progress stored server-side
- Chat messages or moderation records
- Purchase entitlements (where required for compliance)

Some data may be retained where required by law, security, or platform obligations.

---

## 2. How to Request Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us using one of the following methods:

- Email: **(add support email)**
- In-app support or contact form (if available)

Please include:
- Your in-game username or user ID
- The platform you use (Android / iOS)
- A clear request for data deletion

---

## 3. Verification

To protect user privacy, we may need to verify ownership of the account before processing a deletion request.

---

## 4. Processing Time

We will make reasonable efforts to process valid data deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe, typically within **30 days**, unless a longer period is required by law.

---

## 5. Effects of Deletion

Once your data is deleted:
- Gameplay progress may be permanently lost
- Purchases and entitlements may not be recoverable
- Account access may be disabled or removed

Deletion is irreversible.

---

## 6. Legal and Operational Retention

Certain data may be retained if required for:
- Legal compliance
- Fraud prevention
- Security and abuse mitigation
- Platform requirements (e.g., purchase records)

Such data will not be used for gameplay or analytics purposes.
