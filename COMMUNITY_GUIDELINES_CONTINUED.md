COMMUNITY GUIDELINES / CODE OF CONDUCT

Idle Devotion
Last Updated: January 2026

Purpose

Idle Devotion is intended to be a fair, respectful, and safe environment. These Community Guidelines apply to all users and all features, including chat, usernames, and gameplay interactions.

Age Requirement

Idle Devotion is intended for users 17 years of age or older. Do not use the App if you do not meet this requirement.

Allowed Behavior

You are welcome to:

Communicate respectfully with other players

Discuss gameplay, strategies, and community topics

Report bugs or suspicious behavior

Participate fairly in all game systems

Prohibited Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited:

Harassment & Abuse

Hate speech, slurs, or discriminatory language

Threats, intimidation, or harassment

Sexual exploitation or explicit sexual content

Cheating & Exploitation

Using bots, scripts, automation, or macros

Exploiting bugs or unintended mechanics

Attempting to manipulate server data or outcomes

Impersonation & Fraud

Impersonating staff, moderators, or other users

Attempting to deceive others for personal gain

Disruptive Conduct

Spamming, flooding chat, or advertising

Posting malicious links or malware

Encouraging others to violate these rules

Usernames & Profile Content

Usernames, avatars, and profile content must not contain:

Hate speech or sexual content

Impersonation attempts

Offensive or misleading material

WBZK LLC reserves the right to rename or remove content at its discretion.

Moderation & Enforcement

WBZK LLC may take the following actions without warning:

Message deletion

Temporary or permanent mute

Account suspension or ban

Account freeze (read-only mode)

Token revocation or logout enforcement

Moderation decisions are final.

Reporting

Users may report:

Harassment or abuse

Cheating or exploitation

Inappropriate chat messages

Reports are reviewed server-side and logged for moderation and security purposes.

Appeals

At this time, moderation actions may not be appealed. This may change in future versions.

Changes

These Guidelines may be updated as the community evolves. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of updates.
