# Privacy Policy for Idle Devotion

**Last Updated:** January 2026

Idle Devotion (“Idle Devotion”, “we”, “our”, or “the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the App.

By installing or using Idle Devotion, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

Idle Devotion does **not** require users to provide personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or physical address to play.

If optional account or authentication features are enabled, we may collect:

* A unique user identifier
* Authentication credentials or tokens (managed securely)

---

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect limited, non-personal information, including:

* Device type, operating system, and app version
* Anonymous device or installation identifiers
* Gameplay progress and feature usage
* Crash reports and performance diagnostics

This information is used solely to operate, maintain, and improve the App.

---

### 1.3 In-App Purchases

If Idle Devotion offers in-app purchases:

* Payments are processed entirely by the platform provider (Google Play Store or Apple App Store)
* We do **not** collect, store, or process payment card or billing information
* We may receive purchase or entitlement confirmation to unlock content or features

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

* Provide and maintain core game functionality
* Save and synchronize gameplay progress
* Validate purchases and entitlements
* Improve game balance, performance, and stability
* Detect crashes, bugs, and abusive behavior
* Comply with platform and legal requirements

We do **not** sell personal data.

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We may share limited data with trusted third-party service providers strictly for:

* Analytics and performance monitoring
* Crash reporting
* Purchase validation and fraud prevention

These providers process data according to their own privacy policies and are limited to what is necessary to provide their services.

We do **not** share data for advertising or marketing purposes unless explicitly disclosed.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

* Data is stored using reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards
* Sensitive operations (such as purchase validation) are handled securely and server-side where applicable
* Access to data is restricted to authorized systems only

While no system can guarantee absolute security, we take appropriate measures to protect user data.

---

## 5. Children’s Privacy

Idle Devotion is **not intended for children under the age of 13**.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If such information is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

---

## 6. User Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

* Request access to data associated with your account
* Request deletion of stored data
* Opt out of certain data collection where supported

Requests can be made using the contact information below.

---

## 7. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to:

* Provide the App and its services
* Fulfill legal, security, and operational requirements
* Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Data may be deleted when it is no longer required.

---

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated “Last Updated” date.

Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
