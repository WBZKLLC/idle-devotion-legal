DATA DELETION REQUEST POLICY (DRAFT)
Overview

Idle Devotion allows users to request deletion of their personal account data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

What Data Can Be Deleted

Upon verified request, we will delete:

Account credentials

Progression data

Chat history associated with the account (except where retention is required by law)

Identifiers tied to your account

What May Be Retained

Some data may be retained for:

Fraud prevention

Security audits

Legal compliance

Retained data is access-restricted and not used for gameplay.
