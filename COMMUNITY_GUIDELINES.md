# Community Guidelines for Idle Devotion

**Last Updated:** January 2026

Idle Devotion includes community features such as chat. These Community Guidelines exist to ensure a safe, respectful, and enjoyable experience for all players.

By using any community or chat feature in the App, you agree to follow these Guidelines.

---

## 1. Respectful Conduct

You must treat other players with respect.

The following behavior is **not permitted**:
- Harassment, bullying, threats, or personal attacks
- Hate speech or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or similar characteristics
- Sexually explicit, pornographic, or obscene content
- Encouraging self-harm, suicide, or violence
- Impersonating others or spreading false information
- Spamming, flooding chat, or disruptive behavior

---

## 2. Illegal and Harmful Content

You may not use chat or community features to:
- Promote or engage in illegal activities
- Share malware, exploits, or instructions to bypass security or purchases
- Distribute copyrighted material without permission
- Share personal or private information (your own or others’)

---

## 3. Moderation and Enforcement

Idle Devotion reserves the right to:
- Monitor chat and community activity
- Remove content that violates these Guidelines
- Mute, suspend, or permanently ban accounts for violations
- Take action without prior notice when necessary to protect users or the App

Moderation actions are taken at our discretion and may be automated or manual.

---

## 4. Reporting

Players may report inappropriate behavior through in-app tools (if available) or by contacting support.

We encourage users to report violations so we can maintain a safe environment.

---

## 5. Changes to These Guidelines

We may update these Guidelines from time to time.  
The “Last Updated” date will reflect the most recent changes.

Continued use of community features after updates constitutes acceptance.
